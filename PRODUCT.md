# Ritatu Product

Ritatu is a mobile macro tracker for people who want fast meal logging without giving up control over weight and nutrition assumptions. The app serves everyday nutrition tracking, not medical diagnosis.

Register: product

## Users

- People tracking calories and macros daily.
- Users who scan packaged products, photograph meals, or enter food manually.
- Users who need quick correction before save, especially portion weight.

## Product Principles

- The user confirms weight before every meal is saved.
- Calories are calculated deterministically from protein, carbs, and fat.
- AI may estimate food and macros per 100 g, but never owns final calories.
- Logging must feel fast, clear, and trustworthy.
- Error states should guide users back to manual entry instead of dead ends.

## Tone

Calm, practical, warm, and precise. The app should feel like a focused daily tool with small moments of delight, not a marketing site.

## Anti-References

- No generic purple gradients.
- No heavy dark dashboard look.
- No gamified clutter.
- No hidden assumptions about nutrition data.
